About Zone 29

Zone 29 is a fictional universe written by Smijo P. Abraham where superheroes and supervillains exist. From time manipulation to cosmic adventures, the characters of Zone 29 live in a world of constant conflict and mystery. Zone 29 has superheroes for everyone, whether it be for a child or an adult, Zone 29 has something for everyone.
